  Deep Learning to detect COVID-19 through Chest X-Ray Images

  Microsoft- MMI Automata

Microsoft- MapMyIndia Automata is an automation pipeline that uses NLP and Computer Vision to automate the task of adding a location or reporting an incident on the map across India. This information is crowdsourced in the form of text and images, and the automata verify information using text processing and leveraging cloud vision APIs.   EVDA - Enhance Visibility Decrease Accidents (2018)

EVDA is an anti-collision computer vision framework to avoid mishaps (car, train accidents) that might occur due to low visibility or fog. The video captured in frames was transformed into saliency maps and was superimposed with RGB frames of the video. On these superimposed frames, the YOLO algorithm was applied to detect the object, and the type of object in order to increase the visibility. On top of this, a ZED stereo camera was used to calculate the real-time distance from the object in front, and a cumulative score was provided to detect the object and avoid the collision.

Technologies:Machine Learning, Python, MySQL, Angular, HTML, CSS
