Projects

  Deep Learning to detect COVID-19 through Chest X-Ray Images

This project's goal was to detect COVID19 by training a self-developed and available deep learning models of VGG-16, ResNet-50, and InceptionV3 on Chest X-Ray Images.
The images from the IEEE8023 X-Ray Dataset were classified into four separate classes, namely Normal, Pneumonia Bacterial, Pneumonia Viral, and COVID19.

  Natural Language Processing Data Scrubbing Service

This project's goal was to built a service to identify and scrub the relevant sentences of the document against a list of entities.
The service was built by implementing Named Entity Recognition and Coreference Resolution.

  Financial Derivatives Analyser

Financial Derivatives Analyser is an Angular-Springboot based rest application that can analyze Futures and Options derivatives by deriving maximum possible profits/losses, breakeven points, and generate net payoffs graphs of the trades.
This includes a backend layer built over the implementation of complex financial algorithms to calculate the different financial features.

  DTU Farewell Web Application

2020 which brought COVID Lockdown and restrictions for the graduates, and consequently taking away the opportunity for the Batch of 2020 to have a farewell they deserve.
Turning crisis into opportunity, developed a web application facilitate the farewell for more than 2000 fellow students for the batch of 2020. An opportunity for students to have their profiles, share their memories with each other, voting each other for their title, putting up a pictures on photowall, etc.
This gathered a reach of around 7600 new users, along with 400k page views within a week from the web application's release.
